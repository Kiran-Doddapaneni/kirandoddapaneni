# Persona 1 — SOC Analyst  
**Written by:** Tejas Sonawane  

## Profile
- **Name:** Alex Müller  
- **Age:** 29  
- **Role:** Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst  
- **Location:** Berlin, Germany  
- **Technical Skill Level:** High  
  - Familiar with SIEM tools  
  - Log analysis  
  - Networking  
  - Basic scripting  

## Background
Alex works as a SOC analyst in a mid-sized company where he is responsible for monitoring security alerts and investigating incidents on a daily basis.

## Goals
- Quickly identify high-risk incidents  
- Understand attack patterns through visual relationships  
- Reduce alert fatigue  
- Improve investigation speed and accuracy  

## Challenges
Alex spends most of his time reviewing large numbers of alerts generated by SIEM systems. Many alerts are repetitive or low priority, making it difficult to focus on real threats. Investigating raw logs manually is time-consuming and inefficient.

## How the System Helps
The system helps Alex by:
- Prioritizing high-risk alerts  
- Providing visual attack relationship mapping  
- Reducing unnecessary alert noise  
- Offering clear explanations and automated insights  

## Expectations
Alex values automation but still wants human control over decisions. Time efficiency, clarity, and easy-to-understand explanations are very important to him.

## Profile
- **Name:** Priya Sharma  
- **Age:** 34  
- **Role:** Cybersecurity Engineer  
- **Technical Skill Level:** Very High  
  - DevOps  
  - Scripting  
  - Security architecture  

## Background
Priya is responsible for deploying, configuring, and maintaining SIEM systems such as Wazuh. She works closely with infrastructure, detection rules, and system integrations.

## Goals
- Fine-tune detection rules and logic  
- Integrate new intelligence sources  
- Improve automation workflows  
- Ensure scalability and stability of the system  

## Challenges
Managing large-scale SIEM systems requires continuous monitoring, maintenance, and optimization. Priya needs reliable automation while maintaining visibility into how decisions are made.

## How the System Helps
The system helps Priya by:
- Supporting customizable AI-driven workflows  
- Enabling integration with threat intelligence platforms  
- Automating repetitive response actions  
- Providing audit logs and transparent decision-making  

## Expectations
Priya prefers transparent systems over black-box automation. She values modular architecture, scalability, and integration capabilities more than UI appearance.

# Persona 3 — Cybersecurity Student  
**Written by:** Kiran Doddapaneni  

## Profile
- **Name:** Daniel Weber  
- **Age:** 22  
- **Role:** Computer Science Student  
- **Technical Skill Level:** Medium  
  - Basic Python  
  - Networking  
  - Linux  

## Background
Daniel is a cybersecurity learner who wants to understand how real-world SIEM systems work. Although he has limited industry experience, he is highly interested in threat detection and incident investigation.

## Goals
- Learn how attacks are detected  
- Understand incident investigation workflows  
- Explore relationships between users, systems, and IPs  
- Gain hands-on cybersecurity knowledge  

## Challenges
Traditional SIEM tools are often complex and difficult for beginners to understand. Raw logs and technical alerts can easily overwhelm new learners.

## How the System Helps
The system helps Daniel by:
- Providing visual explanations of attacks  
- Showing relationships between entities using graphs  
- Offering guided insights and simplified explanations  
- Allowing experimentation with simulated attack scenarios  

## Expectations
Daniel prefers systems that explain why something happened instead of only showing technical data. Visual learning and guided exploration help him learn more effectively.
